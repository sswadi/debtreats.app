Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide exclusively to operate Spendly for you:

  • To display your expenses and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or alteration. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces from Google's CDN. Google's privacy policy applies to that request.

We do not integrate with payment processors, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your expense data is never shared with third parties.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies are set. You can clear this cookie at any time by logging out or clearing your browser's cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website.
